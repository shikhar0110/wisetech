Theater Design

Theater design is the art of designing and building spaces for live performances. The goal is to provide optimal conditions for the audience to experience a theater performance while aiding performers in achieving their fullest expression. Theater design can encompass open-air or fully enclosed spaces, temporary structures or permanent buildings. At its core, theater design enances the audience's experience at a performance.
